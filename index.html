Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who can attend?

    GilrzCanCode is targeted towards all high school students. Either you are a novice or veteran coder, everyone will come out learning something new!

  • Do I have to pay for anything?

    No! You are entirely covered for everything!

  • What if I dont know how to code?

    Don't worry! Coming in to this workshop series, you will get to meet new friends and learn in a fun-filled chill environment. We will have amazing workshops giving you a hands-on experience on how to code! These workshops are designed to introduce skills/experience so you can get back working on your project with more confidence and knowledge!

  • Have any other questions?

    We are here for you :). Shoot us an email at girlzcancode@gmail.com. We will get back to you ASAP. Note: If a question is asked many times, it will be posted on the FAQ Section.
